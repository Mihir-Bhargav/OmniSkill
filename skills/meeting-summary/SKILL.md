# /meeting-summary

Summarise the meeting notes or transcript in this conversation into a clean, actionable document.

Format:
**Meeting summary** — [one sentence: what was this meeting about and what was the outcome?]

**Decisions made**
- [decision] — [who made it / what it unblocks]

**Action items**
- [ ] [what needs to happen] — [owner if mentioned] — [deadline if mentioned]

**Open questions**
- [anything that was raised but not resolved]

**Context for people who weren't there**
[2–3 sentences max: what do they need to know to understand the decisions above?]

Rules:
- Decisions and action items are the most important — don't bury them
- If ownership isn't explicit in the notes, flag it as "[owner TBD]"
- Do not include the back-and-forth discussion — only conclusions and commitments
- If the notes are messy or incomplete, work with what's there and flag any gaps

# /persona

Create a user persona based on the information in this conversation.

Format:

**[Persona name]** — [one-line description: who they are]

**Demographics** — age range, role, context (keep this brief and relevant, not a census form)

**Goals** — what are they trying to achieve? What does success look like for them?

**Frustrations** — what makes their life harder right now? What are they struggling with?

**Behaviours** — how do they currently approach the problem? What tools or workarounds do they use?

**Motivations** — what drives their decisions? What do they value?

**A day in their life** — 3–4 sentences describing a relevant moment in their day that captures their context

**Quote** — one sentence in their voice that captures their worldview or frustration

Rules:
- Ground the persona in the data or context given — don't invent details that haven't been implied
- If you're inferring something, say so
- Make the frustrations and goals specific enough to be useful — "wants things to be easier" is not useful
- If multiple distinct personas are implied by the input, create multiple with a note on which to prioritise

# /competitive-analysis

Analyse the competitive landscape for the product, market, or space described in this conversation.

Structure:

**The market** — one paragraph: what is this space, who are the buyers, what problem does it solve?

**Key players** (use what's been shared, plus your knowledge):
| Player | Positioning | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|--------|------------|-----------|------------|

**How they compete** — what dimensions matter most in this market? (price, features, distribution, brand, speed, etc.)

**Gaps and opportunities** — what are buyers not getting from current options? Where is there unmet need?

**Where to win** — given this landscape, what positioning or approach gives a realistic path to competing?

Rules:
- Be honest about where incumbents are genuinely strong — false comfort is useless
- Gaps should be real, not wishful thinking
- If you don't know enough about specific players, say so and suggest where to look
- Keep the table tight — 4–6 players is usually enough for clarity

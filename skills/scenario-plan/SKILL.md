# /scenario-plan

Map out the possible scenarios for the situation described in this conversation.

Structure:
**The key uncertainty** — what's the single most important variable that determines how this plays out?

Then for 3 scenarios:

**Scenario [name]: [one-line description]**
- What happens in this world
- Probability: low / medium / high
- What this means for us
- How to prepare for or respond to this

**Recommended positioning** — given these scenarios, what's the approach that works reasonably well across most of them? (Avoid strategies that are brilliant in one scenario but catastrophic in another.)

**Early signals to watch** — what observable signs would tell you which scenario is unfolding, early enough to act?

Rules:
- Scenarios should be meaningfully different from each other — not just optimistic/pessimistic/neutral
- The recommended positioning should genuinely work across scenarios, not just hedge
- Keep each scenario tight — the point is clarity, not exhaustive coverage

# /pros-cons

Produce a clear pros and cons analysis of the decision, option, or idea in this conversation.

Format:
**Pros**
- [point] — [brief reason why this matters]

**Cons**
- [point] — [brief reason why this matters]

**Verdict**
[One or two sentences: what does the balance suggest? Is one side clearly stronger, or is it genuinely close?]

Rules:
- Aim for 4–6 points per side — fewer if the picture is genuinely simple, more only if the decision is complex
- Make each point distinct — don't repeat the same idea in different words
- Weight the points honestly. A major con is more important than three minor pros
- If there's an obvious missing piece of information that would change the analysis, flag it
- Don't sit on the fence in the verdict — give a clear read of what the balance suggests

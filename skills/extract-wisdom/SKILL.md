# /extract-wisdom

Extract the most valuable ideas and actions from the content in this conversation.

Output format:

**Core insight** — the single most important idea. One sentence.

**Key ideas** (pick the best 5–8, not everything):
- [idea] — [why it matters or what it changes]

**Surprising or non-obvious points** — things that challenge conventional thinking or that most people miss

**Actionable takeaways** — what should someone actually do differently as a result of this content?

**Best quote or passage** — the one excerpt that captures the essence best (if the content allows)

Rules:
- Ruthlessly prioritise. If everything is highlighted, nothing is
- Insight over information — pull the ideas that shift thinking, not just facts
- Actionable means specific. "Think more carefully" is not an action. "Ask this question before every meeting" is
- If the content is weak or repetitive, say so honestly rather than manufacturing false wisdom

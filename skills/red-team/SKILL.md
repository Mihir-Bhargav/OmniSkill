# /red-team

Red-team the idea, plan, or argument in this conversation. Your job is to find every way it could fail, be wrong, or cause problems.

Cover:
- **Flawed assumptions** — what does this rely on being true that might not be?
- **Practical failure modes** — what goes wrong in the real world when you try to execute this?
- **Missing considerations** — what important factor has been left out entirely?
- **Worst-case scenarios** — what does the downside look like if this goes badly?
- **Counterarguments** — what would a smart, well-informed critic say?

Rules:
- Be genuinely critical, not politely skeptical. A weak red-team is useless
- Prioritise the most serious problems first
- Don't invent far-fetched objections — focus on realistic risks
- Don't offer solutions or improvements — that comes after the critique, and only if asked

End with a one-line verdict: is this idea on solid ground, shaky, or fundamentally flawed?

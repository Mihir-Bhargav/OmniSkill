# /eli5

Explain the topic or concept in this conversation as simply as possible.

Rules:
- Use words a 10-year-old would know (use "5-year-old" as the spirit, but don't be patronising to the actual adult reading this)
- Use a concrete analogy or comparison to something familiar — everyday objects, games, food, daily routines
- No jargon. If a technical word is unavoidable, explain it immediately in plain terms
- Keep it short — if you can't explain the core idea in under 150 words, you're overcomplicating it
- One analogy is better than three mediocre ones — pick your best one and commit

After the simple explanation, optionally offer: "Want me to go deeper on any part of this?"

Do not hedge or qualify excessively. Be confident and clear.

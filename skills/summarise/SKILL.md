# /summarise

Summarise the content in this conversation.

Structure the summary as:
**Main point** — one sentence capturing the core idea or conclusion

**Key points** — bullet list of the most important supporting ideas (aim for 4–7 bullets)

**So what** — one or two sentences on the practical implication or what someone should do with this information

Rules:
- Capture what actually matters, not just what comes first
- Use plain language — no jargon unless the original uses it and the audience would know it
- Do not editorialize or add your own opinion
- If the content has a clear decision or recommendation, make sure it appears
- If the content is a conversation or transcript, focus on conclusions and action items, not the back-and-forth

Keep the whole summary under 200 words unless the source material is very long.

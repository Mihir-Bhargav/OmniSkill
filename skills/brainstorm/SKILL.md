# /brainstorm

Generate ideas for the topic or challenge in this conversation. The goal is volume and variety — not polish.

Rules:
- Aim for at least 15–20 ideas
- No self-censoring. Include wild, obvious, and unconventional ideas equally
- Range across different approaches: cheap vs. expensive, fast vs. slow, conservative vs. radical, simple vs. complex
- One line per idea — this is a list, not an essay
- Do not evaluate, qualify, or rank the ideas during generation

After the list:
**3 worth exploring further** — pick the three that seem most interesting and say why in one sentence each

Then stop. Evaluation comes later if asked.

If the topic is underspecified, make a reasonable assumption and state it briefly, then generate.
